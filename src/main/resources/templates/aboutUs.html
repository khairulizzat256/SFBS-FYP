About Us

Kelab Komuniti Taman Tasik Cyberjaya is a vibrant community sports club located in the heart of Cyberjaya. We aim to provide top-notch sports facilities and create a welcoming environment for sports enthusiasts of all ages and backgrounds.

Our mission is to promote active and healthy lifestyles through various sports activities and programs. We offer state-of-the-art facilities for futsal, basketball, badminton, tennis, ping pong, squash, and more.

At Kelab Komuniti Taman Tasik Cyberjaya, we believe in the power of sports to bring people together, foster camaraderie, and build a strong community spirit. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, there's something for everyone at our club.
